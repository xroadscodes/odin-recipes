Parmesan Garlic Linguine

Description: A delicious and filling psata dish, with plenty of kick provided by fresh garlic. Parmesan cheese helps to mellow out the bright garlic flavors and adds a traditional feel to this instant Italian classic.

Ingredients:

  • Linguine pasta
  • Cream cheese
  • Fresh garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Heavy cream
  • Pasta water
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Coarse or flaky salt
  • Freshly ground pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook the linguine to al dente, or softer if you desire.
  2. After straining the pasta, let us sit in the strainer while you lightly brown fresh garlic in olive oil in the still hot pan. Next, add cubed cream cheese, stirring while it melts.
  3. When nearly melted, about 3-4 minutes on medium heat, whisk in heavy cream and pasta water to thin it out a tad.
  4. Lastly, toss with parmesan cheese and hot pasta.
  5. When plated, top with freshly grated pepper and flakey or coarse sea salt
